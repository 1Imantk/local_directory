About BizDirectory NG

BizDirectory NG is a local business and startup directory built for communities across Nigeria. It helps residents discover local services, supports small business owners by giving them online visibility, and demonstrates core computer-science concepts in a real-world Flask web application.

Technical Highlights
  • OOP: Each business is modelled as a Business object with attributes and behaviour methods (get_summary(), to_dict()).
  • Queue (FIFO): A collections.deque tracks the 5 most recently added businesses — new items enter at the back, old ones leave from the front.
  • Stack (LIFO): Deleted businesses are pushed onto a stack so the last deletion can be undone by popping it back off.
  • datetime API: Every registered business is timestamped using Python's built-in datetime module.
  • Flask: Multiple routes handle viewing, filtering by category, adding, deleting, and undoing — all wired to HTML templates via Jinja2.
